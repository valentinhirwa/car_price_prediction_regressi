Dataset features dictionary for Used Car Price Prediction:

- brand: Name of the car manufacturer (Toyota, BMW, Ford, Honda)
- model year: Production year of the car (Integer)
- mileage: Total distance driven by the vehicle (Float)
- fuel_type: Energy source utilized (Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, Electric)
- transmission: Gearbox mechanism type (Automatic, Manual)
- engine_size: Engine volume displacement in liters (Float)
- car_price: Final market price of the car (Target Label, Float)
